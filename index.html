Who we are

TildeSec is a UK-based cybersecurity and digital privacy startup—company limited by guarantee—dedicated to building tools that protect your data, strengthen your skills, and secure your digital life. Our projects are designed for everyone—from social media users to small businesses and NGOs.

Who We Help

Individuals

Social media users, parents, content creators. Tools like MyMark help protect your images and identity.

Communities

NGOs, charities, local groups. We partner with local groups for outreach, training, and incident readiness.

Businesses

SMEs, startups, remote teams. From SME infosec to event control tools like FlowCue, we deliver practical protection.

Education

Schools, universities, vocational training. Inspector's curriculum-aligned labs improve outcomes across multiple age groups.

High-Risk Professions

Journalists, activists, executive roles. OpSpy exposure graphing and MyMark fingerprinting assist journalists, activists, and executives.
